About SERVISUS

Nairobi's Most Trusted German Car Specialists

Founded with a singular mission — to bring European-standard automotive care to Nairobi — SERVISUS Runda SESS Auto Spa has become the trusted workshop for German and European car owners across Runda, Muthaiga, Gigiri, Nyari and Spring Valley.

Our technicians are trained on German diagnostic protocols using manufacturer-spec equipment including ISTA for BMW, VCDS for VW/Audi, and Star Diagnosis for Mercedes-Benz. We don't guess — we diagnose.
